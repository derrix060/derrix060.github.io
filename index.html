ABOUT ME

Who is Mario?

My name is Mário Aprá and I'm from Sao Paulo, Brazil. I'm currently studying System analysis and development at Sao Paulo Technology College. I raised at Guaruja (coast from Sao Paulo), and I'm also surfer.

Education

When I was 16 I moved to SP to take my High School Diploma. A year ago, I entered in Brazilian Army as a cadet and stayed for 2 years. After this, I taked time off and moved back to SP and started study System analysis. In the first semester there, I joined in a code jam team and we was the seccond best group at my College and top 20 from all of Technology Colleges. Then, in the seccond year, my group was the 6th from all. In 2016, my team could win the first Hackathon in SP Technology College with an amazing project: real-time subtitle to help deaf people and e-book creator to blind people.

Work Experience

I start work in IT in 2014 with database and in 2015 moved to Una Capital company. There I was in charge of all IT area, because I was alone. I was hired to create a database for the company, to organize their datas, create automated reports and do maintenance in their website. After this, I improoved the system with integrations using SOAP and REST (in Java and C#). In my last year there, I get Working Holiday Visa in New Zealand, so I decided to leave the company and I and one of their partner hired other person to do my job. To do that, I was responsable for filter candidates, apply some programming tests and for all this process. I teached this guy about finance market, because is from Una area, and implemented a SCRUM process to make softwares in this micro team. Also, I implemented some data extracting using Python, VBA and Java.
